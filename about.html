BIOGRAPHY

Hi, my name is James Hooper. I am an artist at heart, which ultimately led me to my passion in web development and design. After I earned a Bachelors Degree in Graphic Design from the University of Utah, I discovered that I wanted to learn everything there was to designing, building, and creating websites. I began to teach myself, HTML, CSS, and a little bit of JavaScript. As I practiced more often, a light bulb turned on and I began to see how much I geniunely love creating websites.

As the years passed, I decided that I needed a career change. I wanted a formal education and fortunately, Utah Valley University had the perfect Bachelors in Web Design and Development. I'll be finishing up the program this year and I am so excited to be in this field!
